This talk presents a preliminary analysis of the Chord Length Sampling (CLS) method applied to dispersion fuel plates. The CLS method provides an efficient approach for handling double-heterogeneous systems with randomly distributed particles, offering a balance between computational accuracy and efficiency for neutronics calculations.
